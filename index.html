Welcome to build-a-wrestler!

You can choose between 3 different weightclasses: cruiserweight, middleweight and heavyweight.
Cruiserweights are the most nimble wrestlers. They have less health than the others but have a high relevancy due to their impressive athletic feats. They also lose health and relevancy at a slower rate than the rest.
Middleweights does not have the most health or the most relevancy but neither are they the worst. They lose health and relevancy faster than cruiserweights but slower than heavyweights
Heavyweights have the most health, the least relevancy and lose relevancy and health more rapidly than the others.
When you have created your wrestler you can press "heal the wrestler" to give your wrestler 1 more health. You can also press "have a match" to gain 1 relevancy but at the cost of 2 health. Use these skills wisely to keep your wrestler healthy and relevant. When either health or relevancy goes to 0 your wrestler will "die"
